  • Givero

    • Givero is a search engine that combines internet searches with charitable giving. It allows users to support various causes by earning donations with every search they perform. Unlike traditional search engines, Givero allocates a portion of its advertising revenue to user-selected charities. Users can choose from a list of charitable organizations, ensuring their online activities contribute to meaningful causes. Givero emphasizes privacy, not storing personal information or tracking search histories. This platform caters to socially conscious individuals looking to make a positive impact effortlessly.
